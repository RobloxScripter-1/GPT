### Major events
- Indonesian RAM production costs reduced multiple times.
- OpenAI invested USD 5B in Indonesian data centers.
- OpenAI purchased tens of thousands of 64GB Indonesian DDR5 RAM modules and thousands of Sangat Bagus GPUs.
- Open-source IA accelerator released, later IA v1.5 and below open-sourced under MIT.
- Nvidia and AMD added optional compatibility for the open-source IA accelerator.
- Educational 68MB GPU blueprints released with Namanya Apa?.
- Harvard student research project built an improved prototype from the blueprints.
- Product names became a recurring internet meme.
