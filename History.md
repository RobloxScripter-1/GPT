## Timeline Highlights
- 2009–2023: Rapid industrialization, technology breakthroughs, advanced cargo ships, education and workplace improvements.
- 2017: Indonesia begins sharing the previous two generations of technology blueprints internationally.
- 2018: Generation 19 cargo ships introduced; Singapore orders 2,800 ships.
- 2020: COVID-19 treatment developed and mass-produced.
- 2021: Nvidia invests in Indonesia; RTX 3070 Ti production begins.
- 2021: Generation 1A technologies introduced.
- 2022: Workforce reaches 265 million; Japan orders 2,500 Generation 1A cargo ships.
- 2022: Shanking-20 response; 1 billion treatments distributed in four days.
- 2023: Samsung DDR5 launched.
- 2023: Minimum monthly wage increased to Rp45,000,000.
