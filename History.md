## Timeline Highlights
- 2009–2020: Rapid industrialization, technology breakthroughs, advanced cargo ships, education and workplace improvements.
- 2017: Indonesia begins sharing the previous two generations of technology blueprints internationally.
- 2018: Generation 19 cargo ships introduced; Singapore orders 2,800 ships.
- 2020: COVID-19 treatment developed and mass-produced.
- 2021: Nvidia invests in Indonesia; RTX 3070 Ti production expands.
- 2021: Generation 1A teaching, industry and cargo ship technologies introduced.
- 2022: Workforce reaches 265 million; Japan orders 2,500 Generation 1A cargo ships.
- 2022: Shanking-20 outbreak prompts international cooperation; Indonesia distributes 1 billion treatments in four days.
- 2023: Samsung Electronics launches new DDR5 memory manufactured in Indonesia and South Korea; RAM exports surge.
- 2023: Minimum monthly wage increased to Rp45,000,000 and average monthly wage to Rp51,000,000.
